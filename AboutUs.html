FOUNDING HISTORY

Phenyo Bagwasi,a tennis enthusiast and sales professional with extraordinary experience had a vision to create TheTennisboutique in 2025.

The lack of a store that sells tennis essentials all in one place gave her an idea to open her own store.

It started mainly focused on female items encouraging more women to participate in the sport but, has since grown to include some male items.

Supported by the increase of online marketing and selling of goods, this website and accounts on other social media apps were created to increase customer engagement.

The store aims to provide customer satisfaction and loyalty of having all their tennis tools and clothing needs met by one company.

Which is why strive we bring the best one can find, so invest with us today.
